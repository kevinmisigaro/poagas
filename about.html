About Us

Our Story

Our journey starts with the quest to make clean cooking available for all. Poa Gas aims to provide sustainable solutions that improve the quality of life as well as our future generations' lives through better, cleaner safer cooking technologies - bringing them much needed relief from this daily burden.



What Drives Us?

Fueling Sustainable Development By Turning Cooking Into A Healthy Experience.

At Poa Gas, we're motivated by the big picture. We want to be a part of the change—that's why our mission is anchored on making cooking safe and clean for all by adhering to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). While there are many approaches that this noble goal can take, here at Pao Gas, we know one thing for sure—if you improve people's access to clean fuel , they will live longer and healthier.
