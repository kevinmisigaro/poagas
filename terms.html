4.1 Open the PoaGas app or mini app within M-Pesa super app.

4.2 Select the service that best suits your needs, whether it be on-demand delivery for refi ll or new cylinders, or opt for pay-as-you-go services.

4.3 Effortless on-demand delivery, select the brand that best suits your needs for on-demand delivery. Fill in the delivery details, make the payment, and sit back and relax as we deliver the cylinder right to your preferred location.

4.5 To view your order history, navigate to the "History" section.
